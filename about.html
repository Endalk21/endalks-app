About the System

The IOT Campus Field Advisor and Choice System is designed to assist pre-engineering students at Hawassa University in making informed decisions about their academic future.

By providing detailed information about various departments, including curriculum, career prospects, and statistics, we aim to bridge the gap between student interests and academic reality.

This platform serves as a digital guidance counselor, accessible anytime and anywhere, ensuring every student finds their right path in the world of technology and engineering.
